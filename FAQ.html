FAQs

How do I order from the online store of Quality Shop ?

- Select the product of your choice by clicking on it.

- Add the product to your shopping cart by clicking the 'add to cart' button.

- The product is added to your shopping cart, feel free to continue shopping in our online store.

- Enter your personal details and choose a payment method.

- Please review your order on the overview page and click the 'pay' button.

- You will be redirected to a secure environment to pay and finalize your order.

- You will receive an e-mail confirmation of your order.

How do you describe the quality of the product?

Using these perspectives, you can define product quality according to Performance and intended function. Reliability of the product within a specific time frame. Conformity to product specifications.

What delivery services/types of delivery are available ?

We can use JNE or J&T express.

When will my order arrived ?

Delivery takes place within 3 to 10 business days from the date of order. Delivery time may vary according to your destination. During high season, we ask for your understanding as delivery times may be longer due to spikes in order volume.

What if I'm not home when the order arrives ?

If you cannot receive your order, please call us at 021-535210004 or whatsapp +62811-1300-2242 to reschedule a new delivery date. Re-Attendance Fee will apply for the next delivery.

Will I be notified when the item will be delivered ?

You will receive an SMS or call notification before your shipment is delivered on the day of delivery.
